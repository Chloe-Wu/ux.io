This is the sample of the binary chart. How could people use this chart to understand the binary system? Let me show you some examples.

There are two red numbers added into the chart. The red "0" above the "1"means "0x1". The red number"1" above the "2" means "1x2". Therefore, this chart shows:"0x1+1x2=2"

There are three red numbers added into the chart. The red "1" above the "4"means "1x4". The red number "1" above the "2" means "1x2". The red number "0" above the "1" means "0x1". Therefore, this chart shows:"1x4+1x2+0x1=6"
