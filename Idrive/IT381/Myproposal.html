Scope

Problem: What problem does this app solve for the consumer? For the producer? Does it sell an idea or a product?

Since the COVID-19 causes, MFC(a fast food restaurant) develops the appï¼Œand offers online orders and delivery services which can help customers stay home to get their favorite food. MFC can make money also without worried about infection.

Scope: Does the app aim to be a "one stop shop"? Does it give all of the information possible about the idea or product being sold? Any comparisons to other products? Reviews? Illustrations? Graphs?

MFC is not a one-stop shop because it only sells fast food such as hamburgers, French fries, fried chicken, chicken wings, pizza, and so on. Unlike other restaurants that need customers to call the front desk, MFC lets them read the menu and order food on the app. Secondary, many restaurants outsource their delivery services to third-party platforms, and MFC has its own drivers who are trained in contactless delivery. Food information like price, content, picture, the size will be displayed in the app.
